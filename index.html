Welcome to the official website of Rugby Ruggers!! Rugby Ruggers was founded in 2003. It is currently headquartered in Albany, NY. Rugby Ruggers is charged with developing the game on all levels and has over 50,000 active member from the age of 8-35. Rugby Ruggers is governed by a nine member Board of Directors, a Congress representing the membership and a professional staff headed by a CEO.
